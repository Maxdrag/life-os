# Life OS 🎮

> Transform your daily life into an RPG. Level up your real life.

## What is Life OS?

Life OS is an Android app that gamifies personal development. Every action you take in real life — exercise, learning, sleep, focus — earns XP and levels up one of your 6 core stats.

## Features

- 🧠 **6 life stats** — Mind, Body, Wealth, Energy, Social, Focus
- 🤖 **AI-powered daily quests** — personalized to your goals via Groq API
- 📱 **Screen time tracking** — real-time monitoring with smart blocking
- 👟 **Health Connect integration** — steps, sleep, activity
- 🚫 **App blocker** — automatically blocks distracting apps after 2h30
- 🔔 **Smart notifications** — AI-generated motivational messages
- 🔥 **Streak system** — daily consistency tracking
